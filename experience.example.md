# Your Name — Experience Corpus

This is your canonical record of professional experiences and
personal projects. The Shortlist tailor agent reads this
alongside resume/resume.tex to select the most relevant
bullets for each job application.

Write 6-8 bullets per role. Each bullet should be a paragraph
of 2-5 sentences with real numbers, technologies, and outcomes.
Far more comprehensive than your resume.
